About Us

For more than two years now, Poetry for Parkinson's has sought to bring together two worlds that rarely meet: those affected by Parkinson's disease and students (and adults) who seldom know about the condition.

It aims to bridge this knowledge gap that limits involvement and holds back action.

Our Vision

As a student-led organization, we are driven by a commitment to raising awareness and turning it into tangible engagement.

Artistic expression lies at the heart of this initiative, rooted in our belief that words can give way to connection and change. Any submission is a step towards understanding, so all artistic submission and participation are welcome.
